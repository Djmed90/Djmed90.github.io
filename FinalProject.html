Welcome to My Final Project Page!

This project that I have created is a game we called space walls. To play it you have to click and drag the ball to sling shot it. The big number in the backround is how many times you can hit a wall before you lose. So on one sling shot try you have to let it bounce around till 0 then go through the door to get to the next level! Enjoy !
